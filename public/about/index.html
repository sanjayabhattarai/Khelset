Our Story

Khelset was born from a passion for cricket and a need for better tools to manage local cricket matches and tournaments. We understand the challenges of manually tracking scores, managing team rosters, and organizing cricket events.

Our team has worked to create a platform that combines the best of modern technology with the traditional spirit of cricket. Whether you're scoring a friendly match or organizing a competitive tournament, Khelset provides the tools you need.

Technology

Built with modern web technologies and available on multiple platforms, Khelset ensures your cricket data is always accessible, whether you're on your phone, tablet, or computer. Our cloud-based infrastructure ensures real-time synchronization and reliable performance.
