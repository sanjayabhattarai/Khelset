üìä About Nepal Premier League Season 2

The Nepal Premier League (NPL) is the country's premier T20 cricket tournament, featuring eight franchise teams representing different regions of Nepal. NPL Season 2 continues the legacy of bringing world-class cricket to Nepali fans, with enhanced production, better facilities, and more competitive matches.

All matches are played across three major venues: Tribhuvan University International Cricket Ground in Kirtipur, Mulpani Cricket Ground in Kathmandu, and Pokhara Rangashala in Pokhara. The tournament follows a round-robin format followed by playoffs and a grand final.
