Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a team?

After logging in, navigate to the Teams section and click "Create Team". Fill in your team details and add players to get started.

Can I score matches offline?

Yes, Khelset works offline. Your match data will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

Is Khelset free to use?

Yes, Khelset is completely free to use. All features including live scoring, team management, and tournaments are available at no cost.

Which platforms are supported?

Khelset is available on Android (Google Play Store) and Web Browser. Your data syncs seamlessly across all devices.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us at khelset.com@gmail.com with your feedback, bug reports, or feature suggestions. We appreciate your input!
