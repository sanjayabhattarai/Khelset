Introduction

Welcome to Khelset ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Khelset (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information You Provide

When you use Khelset, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Phone Number: Used for authentication and account creation via Firebase Authentication
  • Profile Information: Name, email address (optional), and profile picture (optional)
  • User-Generated Content: Match data, team information, player statistics, scores, and other cricket-related content you create or upload

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in the app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, app crashes, system activity, and other diagnostic data

3. Information from Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure phone number authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: For storing match data, team information, and user profiles
  • Firebase Storage: For storing images (team logos, player photos, match posters)
  • Google Sign-In: For optional Google account authentication

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Account Management: Create and manage your user account
  2. App Functionality: Enable core features like match scoring, team management, and live updates
  3. Communication: Send notifications about matches, scores, and app updates
  4. Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and features
  5. Security: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and security threats
  6. Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase security rules to control data access
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Data Location

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform servers operated by Firebase. Data may be processed and stored in multiple countries to ensure service reliability.

Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  1. With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  2. Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in app operation (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud)
  3. Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  4. Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)
  5. Protection: To protect our rights, property, safety, or that of our users
